Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the Athlete Corner mobile application ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our mobile application (the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you create an account or use our Service, we may collect:

  • Account Information: Email address, name, school affiliation
  • Athletic Data: Performance times, event results, competition history
  • Profile Information: Grade level, athlete status, coach/volunteer role
  • School Information: School name, subscription status

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version
  • Usage Data: App usage patterns, feature interactions
  • Camera Data: QR code scanning for event check-ins (processed locally)
  • Location Data: General location for school discovery (when permitted)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Service Provision: Managing events, tracking athletic performance
  • Account Management: User authentication, subscription management
  • Performance Analytics: Generating reports, calculating splits and rankings
  • Communication: Sending event reports via email
  • App Improvement: Enhancing features and user experience
  • Safety: Ensuring appropriate use and preventing misuse

3. Information Sharing

3.1 With Your Consent

We share athletic performance data and event results with:

  • Coaches and athletic directors at your school
  • Event organizers and meet directors
  • Parents and guardians (for student athletes)
  • Other schools participating in the same events

3.2 Service Providers

We work with third-party services that help us operate our app:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, and analytics
  • Email Services: For sending performance reports
  • Cloud Storage: Secure data backup and synchronization

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication systems
  • Regular security updates and monitoring
  • Limited access to personal information

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 Account Control

  • Update your profile information anytime
  • Control which performance data is shared
  • Manage notification preferences

5.2 Data Access and Deletion

  • Request a copy of your data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Correct inaccurate information

5.3 Permissions

  • Camera: For QR code scanning (can be revoked)
  • Location: For school discovery (optional)
  • Notifications: For event updates (can be disabled)

6. Children's Privacy

Our Service is designed for use by student athletes, including minors under 13. We:

  • Collect only necessary information for athletic tracking
  • Require parental consent for accounts under 13
  • Limit data sharing to educational and athletic purposes
  • Provide parents access to their child's information

7. Data Retention

We retain your information:

  • While your account is active
  • For historical athletic records (as commonly practiced in sports)
  • As required by applicable laws
  • Until you request deletion (subject to legal requirements)

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or via email.

10. Contact Us

Email: aniljavali@gmail.com

11. Additional Information for California Residents

Under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), California residents have additional rights:

  • Right to know what personal information we collect
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of sale (we do not sell personal information)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising CCPA rights

12. Additional Information for EU Residents

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), EU residents have rights including:

  • Right to access your personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
